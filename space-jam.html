SpaceJam: A New Legacy

Client: Microsoft Surface

Space Jam: A New Legacy, was a live action / animated sports comedy film directed by Malcolm D. Lee, and starring NBA Superstar, LeBron James. Fans visited a dedicated Microsoft InCulture page to complete challenges in four skill areas. Each challenge tied back to both Microsoft Surface and Space Jam: A New Legacy brands. Completing all four challenges gives users the chance to enter to win a Grand Prize.
