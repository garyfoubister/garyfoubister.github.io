Tater Treats

Client: Tater Tot Designs

Tater Treats brings together our love of fun, unique games and our namesake's love of fruits and veggies! Navigate a flying Tater Tot through several levels of treats, enemies and retina-resolution landscapes to earn a high score. Whether Tater's in the city, the woods or at the beach, he's always hungry for treats. Tilting your tablet up and down helps Tater eat his favorite goodies, like apples and carrots, so you can collect points. Avoid Tater's natural foes (including a flying cat, of course) or you'll lose points!
